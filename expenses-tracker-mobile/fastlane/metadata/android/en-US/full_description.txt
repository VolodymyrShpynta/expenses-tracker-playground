Private Expense Tracker with Cloud Sync

Take control of your finances without giving up control of your data.

Spendium helps you track expenses, analyze spending, and keep your financial records synchronized across devices using your own Google Drive or OneDrive account.

No dedicated backend servers.
No vendor lock-in.
No unnecessary data collection.

Why Spendium?

Your Data Stays Yours
Your financial data is stored in your own cloud storage account. Choose your preferred provider:
- Google Drive
- Microsoft OneDrive
You stay in full control of your information.

Works Offline
Add expenses anytime, anywhere. Spendium works even without an Internet connection and automatically synchronizes your changes when you're back online.

Automatic Multi-Device Sync
Use Spendium on multiple devices. Changes created on different devices are merged automatically using a last-write-wins timestamp rule, ensuring your data remains consistent everywhere.

Privacy First
Unlike many finance apps, Spendium does not require a central server to store your transactions. Your financial records remain in your own cloud storage.

Powerful Spending Analysis
Understand where your money goes with:
- Spending by category
- Monthly summaries
- Expense trends
- Detailed transaction history

Multi-Currency Support
Track expenses in multiple currencies and view spending in your preferred base currency. Perfect for travelers, expats, and users managing finances across countries.

Multi-Language Support
